Research Experience

Graduate Research Assistant (GRA)

Main Supervisor: Assoc. Prof. Ts. Dr. Ku Nurul Fazira Ku Azir

Role: Designed and developed food spoilage detection methods for Ready-to-Eat Foods spoilage classification by collecting and analyzing dielectric properties data of ready-to-eat (RTE) food samples and training deep learning algorithms (e.g. Recurrent Neural Network (RNN), Long Short Term Memory (LSTM) and hybrid CNN-LSTM).

Final Year Project (FYP)

Main Supervisor: Ts. Dr. 'Aini Syuhada Md Zain

Role: Designed and enhanced a conventional blind walking stick using ultrasonic sensors, light sensor and water sensor to detect obstacles and provide feedback to visually impaired users. GSM also included in this project to monitor the location of the blind walking stick users. Responsibilities include circuit design, sensor integration, coding microcontrollers, and testing the system for accuracy and reliability.

Group Design Project (GDP)

Main Supervisor: Dr. Nur Hafizah Ghazali

Role: Collaborated on the design and development of an intelligent restroom system using IoT technology to monitor cleanliness, usage, and maintenance needs. Responsibilities include contributing to sensor integration, data transmission, and interface development, as well as assisting in coding, testing, and troubleshooting system components. Other tasks include participating in team discussions, documenting progress, and presenting project outcomes, while enhancing technical, communication, and teamwork skills.
